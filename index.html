So What is COVID-19?

COVID-19 is an infectious disease that had it's first case in December of 2019. It became a worldwide pandemic at around mid-January as case numbers started to increase. It reached America in January of 2020. At first, it was just a mild case and nobody really thought that it was any serious, until the disease spread rapidly and there were numerous death cases in the US. Then, the government took action.
